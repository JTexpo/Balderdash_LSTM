How to use this app
Upon refreshing or loading the website, you will be prompted with the word 'Balderdash.' Your role is to guess if the word is a real word, given the definition as help.

Click on either the 'Real' or 'Fake' buttons to submit your guess. Test your word knowledge and have fun!

Depending on whether you're right or wrong, either the AI's or your score will go up. Challenge yourself and see how high you can score!

What is LSTM?
LSTM, also known as Long Short Term Memory, is a type of Recurrent Neural Network (RNN) where the AI leverages previous guesses to influence its future predictions. In this example, the AI uses the preceding letters of a word to make an educated guess about the next letters.

The key distinction between LSTM and a regular RNN lies in how LSTM updates its memory after each iteration. This process causes the AI to assign less importance to older data compared to newer data.

However, it's important to note that AI models, including LSTM, are not perfect. There's a chance for hallucinations, where the model predicts incorrectly. These incorrect predictions can closely resemble the intended direction, underscoring the importance of reviewing and cross-checking information provided by an AI.
